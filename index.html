I'll-Serve-Soup

I'll-Serve-Soup is a kitchen management software that makes the process of figuring out when to buy more supplies more streamline. The kitchen management software allows you to view you supplies on both desktop and mobile devices. When product is low, I'll-Serve-Soup will send you a notification when it's time to order more supplies. I'll-Serve-Soup takes away the headache of monitoring your supplies, and allows for a one stop easy access for this.
